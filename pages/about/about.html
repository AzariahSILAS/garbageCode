Hi my name is Azariah!

I am a self taught programmer and though I've come a very long way, its been an extremely tough journey with countless hours and days of stressing, sometimes over things as simple as know how to study. It doesn't seem like a big deal but trust me, that is the point were many give up on this journey and quit. My goal in this blog is to help create a clear path and be someone who can support others on this difficult journey make their experiences a little easier by passing the lessons I've had to learn the hard way!

How do I intend to do that?

Great question. My plan is to not only give you all the information I've learn over the years in small digestible amounts but I also want to create the path that I would take if I were to start my learning journey over again with the experience I have now.

Things to expect

  • A complete beginner to intermediate course for website development
  • A discard community full of students and teachers to network and learn with/from
  • Hands on experience once your ready with projects that. other members of the community are creating
  • A personal mentor who will help you any and every time your stuck.

Lets not waste anymore time!
